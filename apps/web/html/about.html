Our Story

Reimagining guided health journeys for every Indian woman

Nari Swasthya was created to close the gap between medical information and everyday decisions. We combine evidence-based guidance, culturally aware content, and digital tools so women can navigate pregnancy, periods, fertility, perimenopause, and beyond with confidence.

From tier-1 cities to small towns, our aim is simple: make trustworthy women's health support as accessible as your favorite app, in language and context that feels like a friend, not a textbook.

We believe language should never be a barrier to care. Nari Swasthya is built to speak to you in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, and Marathi, ensuring that complex medical advice is delivered in the mother tongue you trust most, bridging the gap for millions across India.

Journeys, tools & stories

What Nari Swasthya stands for

Our platform blends clinical insight with lived experiences so women can make informed, practical choices every day.

Compassion first

Every journey is personal. We treat symptoms, stigma, and questions with the same empathy you would expect from a trusted friend.

Evidence-based care

All guidance is rooted in clinical guidelines and expert review, translated into simple, action-focused language for women and families.

Quality information

From PCOS and fertility to menopause, we prioritize clarity, accuracy, and Indian context over sensational content or fear-based advice.

Community & support

Women learn best from women. Stories, checklists, and shared experiences help you feel less alone and more prepared at every step.

How we support your health journey

Nari Swasthya brings together journeys, tools, and services so you always know what is happening, what is normal, and what you should do next.

Guided Journeys

Step-by-step pathways for pregnancy, reproductive health, prevention, and menopause—aligned with checklists, reminders, and local realities.

Symptom education

Symptom explanations in plain language, with clear "when to watch" and "when to seek care" guidance so you can act early and confidently.

Bridging offline care

Use our tools to prepare for doctor visits, track questions, and understand prescriptions, making every appointment more productive.
