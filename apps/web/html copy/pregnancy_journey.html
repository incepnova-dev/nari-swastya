💆‍♀️ Pregnancy Wellness Tips

Self-care practices for a healthy pregnancy

🚶‍♀️

Stay Active

30 minutes of moderate exercise most days. Walking, swimming, and prenatal yoga are excellent choices.

✓ Start slowly if new to exercise
✓ Stay hydrated during workouts
✓ Avoid overheating
✓ Stop if dizzy or in pain
😴

Rest Well

7-9 hours of sleep nightly. Use pregnancy pillows and sleep on your left side for better circulation.

✓ Establish bedtime routine
✓ Keep room cool and dark
✓ Limit screen time before bed
✓ Take short afternoon naps
💧

Hydrate

8-10 glasses of water daily. Prevents constipation, reduces swelling, and maintains amniotic fluid.

✓ Carry water bottle everywhere
✓ Flavor with lemon or cucumber
✓ Drink before feeling thirsty
✓ Monitor urine color
🧘‍♀️

Manage Stress

Practice meditation, prenatal massage, and breathing exercises. Join support groups for emotional wellness.

✓ Try prenatal meditation apps
✓ Journal your feelings
✓ Talk to supportive friends
✓ Seek professional help if needed
🚭

Avoid Harmful Substances

No smoking, alcohol, or recreational drugs. Limit caffeine to 200mg (1-2 cups coffee) per day.

✓ Read medication labels carefully
✓ Ask doctor before taking anything
✓ Avoid secondhand smoke
✓ Check food safety guidelines
📚

Educate Yourself

Take childbirth classes, read about labor and delivery, and prepare a birth plan with your partner.

✓ Tour your birthing facility
✓ Learn breathing techniques
✓ Understand pain management options
✓ Practice with partner
